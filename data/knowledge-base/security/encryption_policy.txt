TITLE: Encryption and Data Protection

DESCRIPTION:
Encryption protects data at rest and in transit.

RULES:
- All sensitive data must be encrypted at rest.
- Use TLS for data in transit.
- Manage encryption keys securely.

RISKS:
- Unencrypted data can be leaked or stolen.
- Weak encryption leads to compliance violations.

REMEDIATIONS:
- Enable encryption for storage and databases.
- Use managed key services.
- Rotate encryption keys periodically.

CONSTRAINTS:
- STORAGE and DATABASE must have encryption enabled.
- Public endpoints must enforce HTTPS.
