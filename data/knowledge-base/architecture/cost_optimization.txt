TITLE: Cost Optimization Guidelines

DESCRIPTION:
Optimize cloud usage to reduce unnecessary spending.

RULES:
- Remove unused resources.
- Use right-sized instances.
- Monitor utilization regularly.

RISKS:
- Over-provisioned resources increase cost.
- Idle resources waste budget.

REMEDIATIONS:
- Terminate unused instances.
- Downsize underutilized resources.
- Use auto-scaling.

CONSTRAINTS:
- High waste ratio (>0.7) indicates optimization opportunity.

SIGNAL KEYWORDS:
cost, waste, utilization, optimization, pricing
