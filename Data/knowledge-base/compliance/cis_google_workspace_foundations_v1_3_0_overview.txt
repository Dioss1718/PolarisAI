TITLE: CIS Google Workspace Foundations Benchmark v1.3.0 Overview

DESCRIPTION:
Security hardening benchmark for Google Workspace Enterprise administrative controls.
Defines manual security recommendations organized by admin console areas such as Directory, Apps, Security, and access/data control, using Enterprise Level 1 and Enterprise Level 2 profiles.

RULES:
- Apply only to Google Workspace deployments with an Enterprise license.
- Use Enterprise Level 1 recommendations as the baseline profile.
- Treat Enterprise Level 2 as an extension of Enterprise Level 1, not a standalone profile.
- Validate each recommendation using the benchmark’s documented manual audit steps.
- Preserve documented impact statements before enforcing restrictive settings.
- Implement recommendations through Google Workspace Admin Console paths specified by the benchmark.

RISKS:
- Misapplying controls outside Google Workspace Enterprise may create unsupported assumptions.
- Applying Level 2 controls without reviewing impact may reduce usability or performance.
- Ignoring manual validation steps may lead to false compliance reporting.
- Combining unrelated sections into one retrieval chunk may reduce RAG precision.

REMEDIATIONS:
- Separate benchmark ingestion into overview, section-level, and per-control RAG files.
- Tag each recommendation with its profile applicability and admin console path.
- Preserve manual/automated status exactly as documented.
- Include audit and remediation steps as distinct structured fields in downstream control files.

CONSTRAINTS:
- Scope is focused on Google Workspace Enterprise customer deployments.
- Enterprise Level 2 extends Enterprise Level 1 and is not standalone.
- The benchmark uses manual recommendations in many areas; do not assume automation where not stated.
- Devices section is outside scope for this benchmark; Chrome settings are covered by the CIS Chrome Benchmark and Mobile & endpoint settings are planned for a future benchmark.
- Do not host or redistribute CIS benchmark PDFs on non-CIS-owned sites.
