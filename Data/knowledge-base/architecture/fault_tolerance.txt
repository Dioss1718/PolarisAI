TITLE: Fault Tolerance Strategies

DESCRIPTION:
Fault tolerance ensures system continues functioning during failures.

RULES:
- Design systems to handle partial failures.
- Use retry mechanisms and circuit breakers.
- Isolate failures to prevent cascading issues.

RISKS:
- Cascading failures can bring down entire system.
- Lack of recovery increases downtime.

REMEDIATIONS:
- Implement fallback systems.
- Use monitoring and alerting.
- Enable automated recovery.

CONSTRAINTS:
- Critical nodes must have failover strategies.
- Systems must tolerate node-level failure.

SIGNAL KEYWORDS:
fault, failure, recovery, resilience, retry
