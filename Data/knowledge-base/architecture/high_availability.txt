TITLE: High Availability Architecture

DESCRIPTION:
Systems must remain operational with minimal downtime.

RULES:
- Deploy resources across multiple availability zones.
- Use load balancers for traffic distribution.
- Avoid single points of failure.

RISKS:
- Single node failure can cause system outage.
- Downtime affects SLA commitments.

REMEDIATIONS:
- Use redundant instances.
- Configure auto-recovery mechanisms.
- Implement health checks.

CONSTRAINTS:
- Critical services must have redundancy.
- Production systems must avoid single-instance deployment.

SIGNAL KEYWORDS:
availability, uptime, redundancy, load balancer, failover
